Celery Salad Recipe

Ingredients in order

  1. 1 minced red onions or shallot
  2. half lemon squeezed over onion
  3. 1 teaspoon of celery salt and a quarter teaspoon of salt
  4. 1.5 tablespoon of cane sugar
  5. 2 tablespoons of olive olive oil
  6. 5 stock of celery
  7. 1 block of the finest block of parmesian, shaved, at least 1 x 1 x 1.5 inches
  8. .25 cup of salted and roasted almonds, chopped
  9. 1 slice of rye bread
